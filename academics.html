Academics

Dr V. Rajendran's journey began in the by-lanes of Edayappatty a small village in Namakkal Disrict. Taught in vernacular medium, he never let environment dictate his destiny but seized every spare moment to up-skill himself towards successful education.

A pious and orthodox family from agriculture and husbandry ancestry contributed greatly in reaping achievements throughout his life. A Renowned academician & administrator, possesses more than 30 years of service in Administration, Teaching and Research & Innovations.

He created a legacy that impacted the lives of students and staff of Institutes of higher learning as Professor, Chairman, Dean, Director R&D, President, Member of various councils and academic bodies.

His career accomplishments, managerial and leadership skills, ability to navigate a challenging environment, were instrumental in achieving his designation as the Principal in Dr N.G.P Arts and Science College, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India.

 

Education

Dr V. Rajendran achieved a zenith academic status from Bharathiar University for his D.Sc. in Physics specializing in Biomaterials, is a maverick learner. His academic endeavors are myriad and incessant by holding a Ph.D. from Annamalai University and an M.Tech. in Nanotechnology from Anna University. Professor Rajendran is a versatile academician with cumulative excellence in obtaining M.Phil. in Ultrasonics and M.Sc. from Annamalai University and a B.Sc. from Madras University.

 
 
 

Employment

With dynamism and deferential personality, Dr V. Rajendran, heads as the Vice Chancellor, AMET University, Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India, March 27, 2023 onwards.

His academic virtue and dynamic leadership as a Team Leader (Principal) for 5 years of service promoted Dr. N.G.P. Arts and Science College, Coimnatore, Tamil Nadu in Higher Education as one of the Premier Institutions in India.

His erudite organizational personality is prominent with his heading as Professor and Director R&D for 12 years at K.S.R Educational Institutions, Tiruchengode, Tamil Nadu.

Dr Rajendran rendered his knowledge excellence as Lecturer, Senior Lecturer, Assistant Professor and Professor for 15 years at Mepco Schlenk Engineering College, Sivakasi, Tamil Nadu.
