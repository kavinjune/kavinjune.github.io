Prof. Dr. V. Rajendran

M.Sc., M.Phil., M.Tech. (Nanotech), Ph.D., D.Sc.


A dynamic, inspiring, and innovative professional, Dr V. Rajendran has established himself as a renowned researcher, proficient academician and influential administrator.
An outstanding administrator with sterling leadership qualities, he made relentless efforts in developing and leading institutions of repute. He epitomised academic excellence and spearheaded the establishment of several Centers of Excellence and laboratories. Widely travelled around the world for research and collaborations, Dr Rajendran has been recognized for his contributions in the field of nano technology.
